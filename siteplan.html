Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to educate people on philosophy in a short and flashy fashion. The goal is to avoid overloading or using excess or fanciful language while communicating the essential concepts in a functional way.

Audience

The audience of this site would be those with an entry-level or sub-entry-level understanding of philosophy that want an introduction into the field without having to slog through material that would otherwise obstruct entry.
